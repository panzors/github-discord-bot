# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 22, 2026**

## 1. Overview

This software does not collect, store, or retain any personal data. However, by using this software you acknowledge that certain activity may be logged and that **you use this software at your own risk**.

## 2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not:

- Store user data, messages, or identifiers
- Maintain databases of personal information
- Track usage across sessions
- Share any data with third parties for commercial purposes

## 3. Request Logging

**Be aware:** Requests made through or by this software may be logged. This includes but is not limited to:

- API calls to third-party services (e.g., GitHub, Discord)
- Network requests and associated metadata (IP addresses, timestamps, endpoints)
- Logs generated by third-party platforms outside of our control

Logging may occur at multiple levels — by third-party services, your network, your hosting provider, or your operating system — and is outside the authors' control.

**Use this software at your own risk.** Do not transmit sensitive, confidential, or personal information through this software if you are concerned about logging.

## 4. Third-Party Services

This software integrates with third-party platforms such as GitHub and Discord. Those services have their own privacy policies and data practices. We have no control over what they log, collect, or retain. Review their privacy policies before use:

- [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
- [Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)

## 5. Credentials and Secrets

Any API tokens, secrets, or credentials you configure are your responsibility. The authors are not liable for exposure of credentials resulting from misconfiguration, third-party logging, or your own environment.

## 6. No Guarantees

We make no guarantees about the privacy or security of any data transmitted through or processed by this software. If privacy is a concern, audit the source code and operate in a controlled environment.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time without notice. Continued use of the software constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
